About Me

My name is Pranjal Pangging, and I was born on July 21, 2009, in North Lakhimpur, Assam, India. I belong to the Mising community, which is a part of the greater Tani ethnic group. The Tani people believe they are descendants of Abotani and are divided into various groups such as Adi, Apatani, Galo, Mising, Nyishi, and Tagin. I strongly believe in Tani unity and the shared cultural heritage of our people.
